Neutral, Peaceful, Democratic

The Democratic Republic of Pigeonaria conducts its foreign relations based on constitutional principles of neutrality, peaceful cooperation, and respect for international law. Our foreign policy reflects our commitment to democracy, human rights, and multilateral diplomacy.

üìú Treaty Framework

All international treaties must comply with our Constitution and receive approval from the Council of Wings. Treaties that conflict with our current neutrality stance or democratic principles require additional public consultation.

Treaty Requirements

  • Must respect Pigeonarian sovereignty and constitutional principles
  • Military alliances or commitments that alter our neutrality require referendum approval
  • Must be approved by Council of Wings majority vote
  • Subject to Constitutional Court review for compliance
  • Can be terminated if they violate our constitutional principles
